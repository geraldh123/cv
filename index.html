Intro

Hi there! My name is Gerald Huang, a final year undergraduate student at the University of New South Wales in the field of Computer Science and Mathematics.

Education

B.Sc. in Computer Science with Honours, B.Sc. in Mathematics (Pure Mathematics) with Distinction, University of New South Wales, 2018 – 2024.

Research Interests

I am a theoretical computer scientist with deep interests in computational complexity theory, algorithm design, number theory, combinatorics, and cryptography. I am currently authoring two books: An Invitation to Algorithm Design and Analysis and An Invitation to Combinatorics. See here for some of my work!

Teaching

I have previously taught at the undergraduate level in algorithm design, theory of computation, and have mentored students in the capstone computer science project. See here for more details!
